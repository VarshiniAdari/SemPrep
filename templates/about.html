Welcome to SemPrep 🎓

SemPrep is your smart semester preparation assistant powered by PrepGenie — an AI model that helps you generate clean, structured notes from your syllabus text.

How to Use:

  • 📝 Sign up or log in with your credentials.
  • 📚 Enter your syllabus and recommended textbooks.
  • 🤖 PrepGenie will generate detailed notes for each topic.
  • 📂 Your notes will be saved and organized into folders in your dashboard.

Built for students, by students — simplify your study prep with AI!
